Our Story

Where the world's flavours meet under one roof

Café Sandoz is Gurgaon's first and most celebrated international café — a destination where cultures collide beautifully on your plate. We've crafted a space that feels as welcoming at noon as it does electric at midnight.

From hand-rolled pasta to aromatic biryanis, delicate dim sum to creative cocktails — every dish is an invitation to travel without leaving your seat.
